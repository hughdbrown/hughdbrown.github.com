K% = ( (b+1) * p - 1) / b
   = ( b*p - (1-p) ) / b

Win probability (p): The probability that any given trade you make will return a positive amount.

Win/loss ratio (b) or odds: The total positive trade amounts divided by the total negative trade amounts.
