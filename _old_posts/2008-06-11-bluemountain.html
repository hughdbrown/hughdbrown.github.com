I interviewed at BlueMountain Capital Management. Not my worst interview ever, but I was really out of practice.

Questions included:

  • Design a library lending system
  • What are weak references?
  • What is a hashtable? Can any object be a key? Can any object be a value? Can a mutable object be the key?
  • What is the difference between an interface and an abstract class in C#?
  • Calculate the recovery rate for a CDS at par with one period remaining.
  • Implement a deep copy routine for an object that may have cycles or self-reference.
  • Write a routine to calculate the minimum depth of a leaf node in a binary tree.
  • What is the complexity of a hashtable search? What is the complexity in the degenerate case?

I met with Steve, Axel, and Perry Vais.
