I recently visited my 6 year old nephew, and I thought I'd report back on the significant markers in a young boy's world:

  • police cars
  • snakes and spiders
  • sharks and alligators
  • tornadoes, earthquakes, and hurricanes
  • sandcastles and beaches
  • rocks
  • Lego
  • macaroni and cheese, pizza
  • ice cream and cookies
  • dinosaurs
  • Spiderman and Batman
  • chocolate milk
  • ants

So if you can just talk about these things, you'll certainly keep his attention.
