Cut Losses Quickly To Preserve Your Cash

[...]Investors holding on in hopes of a quick turnaround should remember the cardinal rule of CAN SLIM investing: Always sell when a stock drops 7% or 8% below your purchase price. No exceptions.

Why sell so quickly? So you can survive to invest another day.

Protecting capital should top every investor's priority list. It takes discipline, particularly when you've worked hard to research a stock.

You might be tempted to ride out a loss in the hopes of making your money back. But market history is rich with examples of stocks that crushed portfolios when investors failed to cut losses quickly.
